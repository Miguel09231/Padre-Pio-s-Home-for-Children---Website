A. BACKGROUND AND SIGNIFICANCE OF THE STUDY

In our Community Food Drive, we are determined to give adults, teenagers and children the proper nutrients in a 3 day meal. Of course, as a group of students, we are taught to save the environment as well, therefore composting environmentally friendly plastics and packages can bring an array of benefits to the community and the environment. Benefits such as reducing the number of food waste going to landfills could help the environment be more affected by greenhouse gasses and reduce the scent of the landfills, which would usually affect daily life. Other benefits such as improving food security, a greener environment, and advertising social inclusion would help improve the community. But how can PPHC, our partnered institution, make use of our project which is the GR1A Food Drive? With the GR1A Food Drive, it will help the children to be motivated and inspired that there is someone who donated for them. This project can give the children a stock of food and counters hunger. We have thought of having a food drive, in order for the community and PPHC to benefit from it. This Food Drive requires human power, money, supplier, resources and many more. Through this food drive, we are able to help orphans and people in our community, because once we have extra food left from the food drive, we can be able to give it to the orphans in the PPHC, and even if we do not have extra, we will provide them some healthy snacks (Bread, Biscuits, Vegetable chips, etc.) until we are able to do another food drive. But how is our food drive and The PPHC related to our research? Well, since our partnered institution is about Padre Pio caring for Orphans without a home or parental figure to watch over them, and our food drive is providing consumable and recyclable goods to the community due to food shortage, we thought that, since the PPHC is close to our school, The institution may not have the budget for food for the children, as we assume that there are many orphans who are in need of our help due to their improper diet.

B. Statement of the Problem

How does finding an effective food source or how does providing funds help a community facing food shortages.

C. Hypothesis

If we are able to achieve the sufficient funds needed for the drive, we will be able to help supply a community with enough food for their families.
